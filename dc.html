User Manual

Welcome to Divine Counter, your professional dashboard for spiritual tracking.

1. Chanting Controls

Increment your count by tapping the large center circle. A soft chime plays every 108 taps.

  • Desktop: Press Space or Enter to count.
  • Mobile: Uses haptic feedback (vibrations) on supported devices.

2. Adding a New Mantra

To track multiple practices:

  1. Open the Manage Mantras menu (list icon in header).
  2. Look for the "Initialize New Practice" section at the top.
  3. Enter the Mantra Name and your Daily Goal (e.g., 108 or 1008).
  4. Click "Add". The new mantra will become active immediately.

3. Removing a Mantra

To keep your list clean:

  1. Go to the Manage Mantras menu.
  2. Find the mantra you wish to remove.
  3. Tap the Trash Icon once. The button will change to "Del?".
  4. Tap it a second time within 3 seconds to confirm permanent removal.
  5. Note: You must have at least one mantra; the app will not let you delete the final entry.

4. Resetting Data

Each mantra has three layers of reset in the management menu:

  • Reset Round (Chart Icon): Zeros out the current "Session" count only. Total and History are safe.
  • Reset Total (Bars Icon): Clears the "Mala Rounds" and "Total Taps" count for starting a new grand cycle. Daily history logs are preserved.
  • Wipe All (Trash Icon): A complete factory reset for that specific mantra (erases everything).

5. Reports & Themes

  • History: View daily performance, completion percentages, and achievement streaks.
  • CSV Export: Generate a full report of all mantras to open in Excel or Google Sheets.
  • Appearance: Toggle between Pure Light and Divine Dark via the Sun/Moon icon.
